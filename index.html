About Me

How on earth is optometry and coding the same?

It's in the details...
Noticing the slightest tear in the retina - finding a syntax error in a sea of code.

It's about creative problem solving...
Finding a way to correct a patient's sight - trying multiple ways to fix broken code until it works.

It's about teamwork...
Working together to ensure the highest level of patient care - working together to build a project.

For the last five years, I've had the opportunity to work up close and personal with an amazing team of healthcare professionals. But as priorities in life change, so do careers. I was looking for a new path that would bring more stability and more of an intellectual challenge. I gravitated towards software development because the idea that technology can be used to improve so many aspects of life excites me. Every day brings on new challenges and I look forward to continue to explore the lifetime of learning this new career brings.
